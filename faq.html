Social Media Growth

Buying followers, likes, views and more.

01Is buying followers safe for my account?+

Yes. We only use your public profile URL — no password, no login access. Our methods are compliant with platform terms and use real, human-looking accounts. We never ask for credentials and we never access your account directly.

02How fast is delivery?+

Delivery starts within minutes to hours depending on the platform and service: Instagram Followers start in 1–4 hours, TikTok Views start in 30 minutes, YouTube Subscribers in 24–72 hours, Spotify Plays in about 1 hour. All timelines are confirmed before payment.

03Do I need to share my password?+

Never. We only need your public profile or post URL. No password, no login, no access to your account — ever. If any service asks for your password, it is not LocaBey.

04Will the followers drop after delivery?+

We offer a 30-day drop protection guarantee. If your follower count drops within 30 days of delivery, we top up for free. Just message us on WhatsApp with your order details.

05Are the followers real?+

We offer real and realistic accounts depending on the package. Our standard packages use high-quality accounts with profile pictures and activity history. Premium packages include targeted followers from specific regions or niches — confirm your preference on WhatsApp.

06Which platforms do you support?+

Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, X / Twitter, Spotify, Snapchat, Twitch, Telegram, and LinkedIn. If your platform is not listed, ask us on WhatsApp — we add new platforms regularly.

07Can I order a custom quantity?+

Yes. Every service has a "Custom" option in the builder. Type any number and it will be included in your WhatsApp order. We quote custom quantities manually and confirm the price before delivery starts.

08Can I choose how fast delivery happens?+

Yes. You can choose "Immediate 24h" for the fastest delivery, or "Custom" to spread the delivery over several days or weeks. A gradual drip looks more organic and is better for accounts that want to avoid sudden spikes.

Orders & Payment

How the WhatsApp checkout works.

Because we verify every order manually before charging anything. A human checks your URL, confirms the price, and answers any questions. This eliminates mistakes, scams and bad deliveries. You only pay after confirmation — no surprises.

After confirmation only. You send your order via WhatsApp, we review it and confirm the details, then we send you a payment link. Nothing is charged before you approve the final order.
