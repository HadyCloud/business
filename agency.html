Quick answers before we start working together.

01How fast can you build a website?+

Simple websites can be live in days when assets are ready. Complex projects take longer — we always agree on a clear timeline before starting.

02Can I start without photos, logo or content?+

Yes. We can shoot photos, design a logo, write copy or organize what is missing. Starting with nothing is fine — we build everything together.

03Can I choose only one service?+

Absolutely. Start with a logo, a website or a content pack. Add more later when ready. No bundle required.

04How does pricing work?+

Every project is quoted after a short call. We send a clear proposal with scope, timeline and price before any work starts. No surprise invoices.

05Do you work on-site or remotely?+

Both. We are Paris-based and can meet in person for shoots, kick-offs or reviews. Everything else is handled remotely and fast.

06Can I add more services after we start?+

Yes. Most clients start with one service and expand over time. A website often leads to content, then ads, then full monthly management.

07How do I track progress during the project?+

We share updates via WhatsApp or your preferred channel. You review and validate at each step — nothing goes live without your approval.

08What happens after the project is delivered?+

We don't disappear. Adjustments and small updates are part of the handover. Long-term support is available through a monthly plan.
